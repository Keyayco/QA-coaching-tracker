About this workspace.

QA Command Centre reads and writes directly to this Google Sheet. There is no separate database — every value shown here is calculated live from the Departments, Agents, Coaching Log, Performance Log, and Dispute Log tabs.

Individual audit records remain the responsibility of Zendesk, Talkdesk, and D2C reporting — this tool only stores consolidated weekly performance, coaching sessions, and disputes.
